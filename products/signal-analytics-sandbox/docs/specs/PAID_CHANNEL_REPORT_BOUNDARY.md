# Paid Channel Report Boundary

Date: 2026-05-22
Status: `phase36_boundary_v1`

This boundary separates a compact dashboard from a paid deep report.

## Public/Free Dashboard

Allowed only after external gate approval:

- channel identifier;
- evaluated source window;
- sample size and confidence labels;
- compact aggregate metrics;
- high-level blockers and limitations;
- safe selected excerpts;
- external gate state.

The dashboard must not imply investment advice, future profit, or universal
best-channel ranking.

## Paid Deep Report

The paid report may contain:

- full claim ledger;
- evidence appendix;
- confirmed and contradicted examples;
- counterexamples;
- source coverage gaps;
- media linkage and transcript/OCR status;
- provider/proxy decisions;
- methodology and risk-management notes;
- trend sense and insight-depth analysis;
- practical-usefulness and creativity examples;
- why a channel is useful, limited, or not defensible.

## Always Blocked

- private Telegram scraping;
- paywalled/login-walled/access-control bypass evidence;
- investment advice;
- future-profit claims;
- unsupported author ranking;
- unaccepted media/OCR/chart claims;
- provider gaps counted as losses.

## Gate Rule

Every public dashboard and every paid deep report needs an explicit
external-ready gate. If the gate says internal-only or rejected, the artifact can
support development and buyer discovery only.
