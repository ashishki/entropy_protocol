# ICP Demo Variants RU

Использовать эти варианты для founder-led demo. Не делить product
implementation по ICP, пока real outreach evidence не покажет, какая группа
конвертит. Во всех вариантах продукт один и тот же: post-trade audit, no broker
control, no signal analytics, not investment advice, does not control live
trading и no order blocking.

Каждый вариант идет в same validation evidence gate, not vanity metrics:
10 qualified prospects, 5 exports/rules, 3 paid audits и 2 repeat audit
commitments.

## Prop/Funded Traders

- ICP: prop/funded traders.
- Likely pain: payout risk, max daily loss, drawdown, cooldown и forbidden
  behavior rules могут закрыть account, даже если trader считает, что mostly
  followed the plan.
- Current workaround: platform dashboard, spreadsheet, journal screenshots или
  coach review after the fact.
- Demo angle: показать source row ids, repeated rule breaches, affected P&L и
  limitations из deterministic report вместо subjective recap.
- Required proof: real trade export, written prop/funded rules и operator
  approval для ambiguous rule mapping.
- Paid pilot ask: купить один manual audit за последний export period и решить,
  нужен ли repeat audit перед следующим payout window.

## Active Crypto Discretionary Traders

- ICP: active crypto discretionary traders.
- Likely pain: fast sessions, multiple symbols, revenge trades, oversized
  positions и слабый cooldown discipline трудно восстановить из exchange export.
- Current workaround: exchange CSV, manual notes, screenshots, TradingView
  annotations или private journal.
- Demo angle: сравнить raw export rows с deterministic rule checks, source row
  ids, violation-attributed P&L и concise next-review checklist.
- Required proof: real trade export, written risk rules, session timezone и
  account currency без API keys или wallet/broker credentials.
- Paid pilot ask: оплатить один manual audit и проверить, находит ли report
  discipline breaks быстрее текущего journal.

## Small Teams/Coaches

- ICP: small teams/coaches.
- Likely pain: coach или team lead нужен consistent review across traders без
  чтения каждой private note и без ручного придумывания rule interpretations.
- Current workaround: shared spreadsheets, screenshots, Notion pages, group
  calls или ad hoc manual review.
- Demo angle: показать, как same policy template дает per-trader reports с
  source row ids, rule breaches, limitations и repeatable evidence.
- Required proof: one anonymized team/trader export, written team rules, privacy
  agreement и explicit operator approval для policy mapping.
- Paid pilot ask: оплатить small manual audit pack для one to three traders и
  решить, стоит ли scheduling repeat audits или referrals.

## Boundary

Это positioning variants only. Они не создают separate products, dashboards,
broker integrations, signal analytics или advice flows. Использовать same
validation evidence gate и evidence log для всех variants.
